What is meant by 'Shared' and 'Private'?

When you purchase a 'Shared' account plan, you will be sharing it with the account owner. You are not allowed to change anything with the account in such plans. On the other hand, 'Private' accounts are fully yours and you are free to change it as per your liking.

What if I do not receive anything upon payment?

You will instantly receive the login details to your Netflix subscription once Sellix confirms the payment. The time to confirm can be greater for cryptocurrency transactions.

What If I lose access to my account after some days?

If the account access is lost, you will be provided a brand-new account, free of cost. Simply join our Discord server and follow the instructions in the replacement channel.

How do I know this isn't a scam?

It sounds too good to be true, but it is. Don't take our word though, head over to our Discord server and view the vouches channel to see all the reviews our customers have left.
