Is the payment safe? Is there any chance of a data breach?

All the payments are processed by our trusted e-commerce partner, Sellix. It is used by millions of people, every single day. All your data is saved securely in their servers and in case of any issues regarding a payment, 24/7 customer support is available.

What if I do not receive anything upon payment?

You will instantly receive the login details to your Netflix subscription once Sellix confirms the payment. The time to confirm can be greater for cryptocurrency transactions.

If the account access is lost, you will be provided a brand-new account, free of cost. Simply join our Discord server and follow the instructions in the replacement channel.
